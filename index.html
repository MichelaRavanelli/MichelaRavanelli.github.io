About me

Ciao! I am Michela Ravanelli, a researcher specialized in Geodesy, GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System), and ionospheric seismology, with a particular focus on tsunami early warning systems. Currently, I am a recipient of a research grant from the AXA Research Fund in collaboration with IOC-UNESCO, focusing on improving tsunami early warning systems through the advanced use of GNSS
